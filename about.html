About Us

Crichton Intellectual Property Consultant was established with a vision to deliver exceptional intellectual property and regulatory compliance services to meet the evolving needs of businesses and innovators. Our firm is dedicated to protecting and managing intellectual assets through innovative, cost-effective, efficient, and practical intellectual property solutions. In addition, we assist in ensuring that businesses are duly formed and operated in accordance with the relevant regulatory requirements and continuing obligations.

We specialize in filing and managing trademarks, patents and copyrights, and facilitate all necessary registrations with the National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and Control (NAFDAC)
